How to Use

Getting Started

When you first open RandomCreator, you'll see the Number Randomizer mode ready to use. Simply tap the "Generate" button to create your first random number!

Number Randomizer

  1. Open the Settings tab at the bottom
  2. Adjust the minimum and maximum values for your range
  3. Return to the Randomizer tab
  4. Select Number mode from the picker
  5. Tap Generate to get your random number

Name Randomizer

  1. Go to the Settings tab
  2. Scroll to the Name Randomizer Settings section
  3. Add names to your list (up to 10 names)
  4. Return to the Randomizer tab
  5. Select Name mode from the picker
  6. Tap Pick a Name to see the roulette animation and result

Dice Randomizer

  1. Go to the Settings tab
  2. Adjust the number of dice and sides per die
  3. Return to the Randomizer tab
  4. Select Dice mode from the picker
  5. Tap Roll Dice or shake your device to roll!

Managing Name Lists

  • Add names: Type a name in the text field and tap "Add"
  • Remove names: Swipe left on any name and tap "Delete"
  • Reorder names: Tap and drag the handles on the right side
  • Duplicate protection: The app prevents duplicate names (case-insensitive)

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is my data private and secure?

A: Absolutely! RandomCreator stores all settings and name lists locally on your device only. No data is ever sent to the internet, collected, or shared with third parties. The app works completely offline.

Q: How do I reset all my settings?

A: Go to the Settings tab and look for the "Reset Settings" option, or simply delete and reinstall the app to start fresh.

Q: Why can't I add more than 10 names?

A: The limit of 10 names is designed to keep the interface clean and ensure the roulette animation works smoothly. This covers most common use cases while maintaining performance.

Q: The shake-to-roll feature isn't working. What's wrong?

A: Shake detection only works on physical iOS devices, not in the iOS Simulator. If you're using a real device and it's not working, make sure you've granted motion permissions to the app. You can also use the "Roll Dice" button as an alternative.

Q: Are the random numbers truly random?

A: RandomCreator uses Apple's SystemRandomNumberGenerator, which provides cryptographically secure random numbers. The results are as random as possible for a mobile app.

Q: Can I save multiple name lists?

A: Currently, the app supports one name list at a time. You can modify your list anytime in the Settings tab. Future versions may include multiple list support.

Q: Does the app require an internet connection?

A: No! RandomCreator works completely offline. All features are available without any internet connection.

Q: Is RandomCreator compatible with VoiceOver and accessibility features?

A: Yes! RandomCreator is fully accessible with VoiceOver labels, Dynamic Type support, and large touch targets for users with accessibility needs.

Q: Will my settings be saved if I close the app?

A: Yes, all your settings, name lists, and preferences are automatically saved and will be restored when you reopen the app.

Q: Can I use this app for gambling or betting?

A: RandomCreator is designed for entertainment, decision-making, and gaming purposes. Please check your local laws regarding gambling and use the app responsibly.

Troubleshooting

App crashes or freezes

  • Force close the app and reopen it
  • Restart your device
  • Make sure you're running the latest version of the app
  • Ensure your iOS is updated to iOS 17.0 or later

Settings not saving

  • Check that you have sufficient storage space on your device
  • Try force closing and reopening the app
  • If the problem persists, delete and reinstall the app

Shake detection not working

  • Confirm you're using a physical iOS device (not simulator)
  • Go to Settings > Privacy > Motion & Fitness and ensure RandomCreator has permission
  • Try shaking more vigorously
  • Use the "Roll Dice" button as an alternative

Can't add names to the list

  • Ensure the name isn't already in your list (duplicates are not allowed)
  • Check that you haven't reached the 10-name limit
  • Make sure the name isn't just whitespace
